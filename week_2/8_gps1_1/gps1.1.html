Key Points of Pair Programming

What are the advantages of pair programming?

  • You can exchange thoughts and ideas with others
  • Having another set of eyes helps prevent mistakes
  • Helps prevent getting stuck on a problem

During a pairing session, there are driver and navigator roles.

Here are some points for a driver:

  • the one who types
  • listens to instructions
  • detail/syntax-oriented

Here are some points for a navigator:

  • gives instructions
  • guides the session to the end result
  • chooses coding style
